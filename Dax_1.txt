Q1. Find the First Purchase Date of Each customer

Scenario - You want to see when a customer first purchased

First Purchase Date = 
	CALCULATE(MIN(Sales[Date]),ALLEXCEPT(Sales, Sales[CustomerID]))

Q2.Show Sales ONly for Last 3 Months

Scenario - Create a measure that shows sales in the last 3 months from Today

Sales Last 3 Months = 
	CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[SalesAmount]), 
	DATESINPERIOD('Date'[Date], MAX('Date'[Date]), -3, MONTH))


Q3. Find the customer with the Highest Purchase in Each Region

Scenario : From Sales table, get the top customer in each region

Top Customer Sales = CALCULATE(MAXX( VALUES(Sales[CustomerID], CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[SalesAmount]))),ALLEXCEPT(Sales, Sales[Region]))


Q4.Calculate Average Sales per Customer

Scenario - Find out the average revenue generated per customer

Avg Sales per customer = 
	DIVIDE(SUM(Sales[SalesAmount]),
	DISTINCTCOUNT(Sales[CustomerID]))
